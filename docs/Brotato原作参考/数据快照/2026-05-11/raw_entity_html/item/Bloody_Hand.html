Item

Bloody Hand is an in-game item that can be bought from a Shop or can be found in a Crate. Most Items modify the Stats of the player, and some of them provide additional effects.

Unique

Bloody Hand is a unique item. Only 1 of each unique item can be equipped, and it will then no longer be offered in the Shop, and no longer drop from loot crates.

Bloody Hand

Bloody Hand Provides a large amount of Life Steal and Damage at the cost of dealing 1 damage to you each second.

Strategy

The self-damage of Bloody Hand triggers on-hit effects of characters such as Bull and Masochist; this can lead to strong synergy.

  • The self-damage will usually kill you if you hold a Weird Ghost, which puts your current HP to 1 at the beginning of the wave.
  • The effect from Tardigrade is wasted by the self-harm.
  • Triangle of Power should not be used with this item, as you would lose 2 %Damage per second.

Trivia

"Bloody Hand" is one of several easter-eggs that represent Creator, who shared Brotato-Gameplay on YouTube or Twitch respectively and thus helped popularize it. The item-icon is a representation of the channel-icon of german YouTube creator "HandOfBlood", whose channel-name itself is a reference to the Song "Hand of Blood" from "Bullet for my Valentine".
