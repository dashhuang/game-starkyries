Item

Sifd's Relic is an in-game item that can be bought from a Shop or can be found in a Crate. Most Items modify the Stats of the player, and some of them provide additional effects.

Unique

Sifd's Relic is a unique item. Only 1 of each unique item can be equipped, and it will then no longer be offered in the Shop, and no longer drop from loot crates.


Notes

Sifd's Relic automatically collects any Materials on the map. This is quite useful for abilities that trigger upon material pickup such as Baby Elephant or Cute Monkey

  • When Cursed, Sifd's gains +10% Dodge, which is then affected by the curse modifier, giving you more than 10% Dodge.


Trivia

The Sifd's Relic is a reference to the Youtuber Sifd, who made the Sifd's Mod for Brotato that was very popular back in the day.
