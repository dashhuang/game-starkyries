Turret

From Brotato Wiki

Turret
Item
Tier 1
Stats
Spawns a turret that shoots bullets dealing 10 (+80%) damage
Price
15


Tags
Engineering, Structure

Item

Turret is an in-game item that can be bought from a Shop or can be found in a Crate. Most Items modify the Stats of the player, and some of them provide additional effects.

Turret

Buying the item spawns a Turret at the beginning of each wave that attacks enemies within 300 range for 10 + (80% ) damage, attacking every 0.73 seconds.

It is identical to the turret spawned by a Wrench Tier 1.

Scaling

Turrets being Structures are unaffected by Primary Stats, such as Damage, Life Steal, Attack Speed, Crit Chance. But are affected by Engineering.


Turrets are affected by Secondary Stats such as piercing and bouncing granted by the items Bandana & Ricochet. Turrets can inflict burning with Scared Sausage, and Snake helps inflict burn to more targets.
