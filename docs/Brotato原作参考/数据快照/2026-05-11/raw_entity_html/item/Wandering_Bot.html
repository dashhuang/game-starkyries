Wandering Bot

From Brotato Wiki

Wandering Bot
Item
Tier 3
Stats
Spawns a little bot that slows down nearby enemies
Price
60


Tags
Less Enemy Speed, Structure

Item

Wandering Bot is an in-game item that can be bought from a Shop or can be found in a Crate. Most Items modify the Stats of the player, and some of them provide additional effects.


Notes

Wandering Bot spawns a small robot that orbits around your character. It slows down any enemies that come close to it, similarly to Taser. Wandering Bot can slow any enemy including Bosses and Elites.

Wandering Bot counts as a Structure and gives +2 Armor/Elemental Damage with Streamer or Technomage.


  • When Cursed, Wandering bot gains +5% Speed, which is then affected by the curse modifier, giving you more than 5% speed.
