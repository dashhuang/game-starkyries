Grenade Launcher

Grenade Launcher is a weapon that can be bought from the Shop. Weapons are primary damage dealers and most Characters can hold up to 6 weapons.

Grenade Launcher is a Ranged Weapon. Ranged weapons shoot projectiles that travel up to the total Range. Unless the weapon has piercing, the projectiles stop at the first enemy that is hit.

Reload

The Grenade Launcher has to reload every time it has fired 2/3/4 shots. This reload time scales with Weapon Tier and Attack Speed.

The average cooldown is: 2.035s/1.31333...s/0.9525s

The listed DPS of this weapon includes the reload cooldown.


Class

Grenade Launcher has the Weapon Class(es) of: Heavy, Explosive.

Weapons in the same class gain bonuses, and are more likely to be found in the Shop.
