Gatling Laser

Gatling Laser is a weapon that can be bought from the Shop. Weapons are primary damage dealers and most Characters can hold up to 6 weapons.

Gatling Laser is a Ranged Weapon. Ranged weapons shoot projectiles that travel up to the total Range. Unless the weapon has piercing, the projectiles stop at the first enemy that is hit.

Notes

Gatling Laser has the fastest possible base attack speed of any weapon, with a base cooldown stat of 2, and a recoil duration of 0.02, giving it a base cooldown of 0.073. This matches Chain Gun and Tier IV Minigun. These three guns have the same attack speed. Combined with its good ranged damage scaling and 3x crit multiplier, Gatling Laser is capable of dealing massive damage.

Class

Gatling Laser has the Weapon Class(es) of: Legendary, Heavy.

Weapons in the same class gain bonuses, and are more likely to be found in the Shop.
