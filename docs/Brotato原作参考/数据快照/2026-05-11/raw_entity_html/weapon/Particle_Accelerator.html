Particle Accelerator

Particle Accelerator is a weapon that can be bought from the Shop. Weapons are primary damage dealers and most Characters can hold up to 6 weapons.

Particle Accelerator is a Ranged Weapon. Ranged weapons shoot projectiles that travel up to the total Range. Unless the weapon has piercing, the projectiles stop at the first enemy that is hit.

Notes

The Particle Accelerator due to its beam style projectile cannot bounce. The tooltip might say bounce if you have Ricochet, but it won't affect the weapon.

Class

Particle Accelerator has the Weapon Class(es) of: Heavy, Elemental.

Weapons in the same class gain bonuses, and are more likely to be found in the Shop.
