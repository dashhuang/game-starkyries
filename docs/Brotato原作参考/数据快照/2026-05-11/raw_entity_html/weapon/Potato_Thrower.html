Potato Thrower

Potato Thrower is a weapon that can be bought from the Shop. Weapons are primary damage dealers and most Characters can hold up to 6 weapons.

Potato Thrower is a Ranged Weapon. Ranged weapons shoot projectiles that travel up to the total Range. Unless the weapon has piercing, the projectiles stop at the first enemy that is hit.

Class

Potato Thrower has the Weapon Class(es) of: Support.

Weapons in the same class gain bonuses, and are more likely to be found in the Shop.
