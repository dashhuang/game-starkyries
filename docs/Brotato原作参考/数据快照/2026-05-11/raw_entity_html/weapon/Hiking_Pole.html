Hiking Pole

Hiking Pole is a weapon that can be bought from the Shop. Weapons are primary damage dealers and most Characters can hold up to 6 weapons.

Hiking Pole is a Melee Weapon. Melee weapons have two different attack types, thrust and swing, both are capable of hitting multiple enemies. Melee weapons' Range only increases or decreases by half the amount of your Range stat. The attack speed of melee weapons depends on their range. Increasing the range slightly decreases the Attack Speed, but increases the area hit by the weapon. Negative range has the inverse effect.

Steps

Hiking Pole's unique mechanic is it's ability to increase character's range (and consequentially Pole's damage) by taking steps during a wave.

Some notes about the mechanic

  • By default character takes 3.33... steps each second while moving through arena.
  • Steps scale with the Speed stat. Having 10 Speed means character walks (3.33... + 10%) = 3.66... steps each second.
  • Walking into a wall doesn't give any steps. Only walking that actually covers distance counts for a step.

Class

Hiking Pole has the Weapon Class(es) of: Support.

Weapons in the same class gain bonuses, and are more likely to be found in the Shop.
