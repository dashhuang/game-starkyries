Crit Chance

Your weapons have an x% increased chance to deal a critical hit. The maximum chance is 100%, meaning every hit is a critical. If this is negative, the value is subtracted from your weapons base crit chance, to a minimum of 0% chance to critical hit.

Most weapons have a 3% base chance to deal a critical hit for 2x the damage. Some weapons have a higher critical hit damage multiplier such as Knife or a special effect on Crit such as Thief Dagger. For these weapons focusing on getting more critical chance is often important.

Some weapons like Fist have a reduced Critical hit Multiplier, dealing only 1.5x damage on a Crit. For these weapons, critical chance is less effective and sometimes skipped entirely.

You can see the critical hit chance and damage multiplier of all weapons at the Weapons page. And you can find a list of weapons with enhanced Critical hits Below

What can Crit?

What can Crit?
Can ✔ Crit Can't ❌ Crit
All hits from Weapons along with any projectiles spawned by those weapons. Structures - Unless you have Pile of Books
Piercing and Bouncing attacks can crit individually on each Pierce/Bounce. Burn
All Explosions. This includes explosions from weapons, such as Rocket Launcher and explosions from Characters such as Bull and Glutton. Explosions from items can crit as well, such as Rip and Tear or Spicy Sauce. The ability from Lucky and Lich
Projectiles from Items such as Alien Eyes and Baby with a Beard can crit with a base 3% chance for 2x damage. Randomly targeted damage from items such as Baby Elephant, Cyberball, or Riposte
