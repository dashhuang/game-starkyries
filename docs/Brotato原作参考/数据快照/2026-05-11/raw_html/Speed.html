Speed

You move x% faster. There is no limit to speed. If it is negative you move x% slower.

Having a higher speed allows you to position yourself for good attacks and evade enemies and their projectiles with more ease.


Character's default speed is 450 units/s. This also means that the character outruns most of the enemies even with no increase to your speed stat.
