Dodge

You have a x% chance to dodge attacks. The limit is 60% chance to dodge an attack. If you have negative Dodge, nothing happens, just as if the stat was 0.

There are two Characters with enhanced Dodge limits:

  • Cryptid with a dodge cap of 70%
  • Ghost with a dodge cap of 90%
