Ranged Damage

Ranged damage is a flat damage stat just like Melee Damage and Elemental Damage. It increases the damage of most ranged weapons. You can find a complete list of weapons that scale with Ranged Damage on the Weapons page.


Negative Ranged damage will instead reduce the damage of your weapon down to a minimum of 1.
