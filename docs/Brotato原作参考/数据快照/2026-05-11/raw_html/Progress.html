New Dawn Challenges

Icon Name Challenge Unlocks Unlock Type Notes
Give the money back Kill the Gobbler 5 times Gobbler's Hat Item The Gobbler appears randomly (3% chance after 10 seconds into the wave)
Ew, what's that smell?! Win a run in Danger 3 or more Ban System Item System, allows you to banish 8 items from the shop per run
Let's try this wave again During the last 5 seconds of a wave after wave 2, press 'Return to Main Menu'. Then press 'Resume' to try the wave again. Hourglass Item
Trick or Treat? Win a run without locking any items in the shop. Candy Bag Item Playing as a character like Gangster who can't lock items will make this simple. Lower difficulty levels also work.
Mmm, so juicy! Collect 100 fruits while at maximum health Fruit Basket Item Characters who benefit from fruit at max health like Glutton or Farmer may get this achievement passively. Also consider Penguin.
Smells like something's burning? Kill 30 enemies by burning them in a single wave Will-o'-Wisp Item A fire-focused Mage build should pick this up easily. Alternatively, as this seems to count enemies who died while on fire, any character with a Scared Sausage and lots of damage should do it.
BooOOooOoo~ Reach 70% Dodge Ghost Outfit Item Ghost is recommended
Crit Happens Reach 100% Crit Chance Vorpal Sword Weapon This is easiest to achieve in Endless mode by going for Crit Chance upgrades after you have a build going, as 100% Crit Chance is much higher than you'd normally aim for in a game.

The Abyss

These challenges are exclusive to the Abyssal Terrors DLC(DLC).

Game Challenges (DLC)

Icon Name Challenge Unlocks Notes and Tips
Smelly Feet Walk a total of 20000 steps
Hiker		 Unlocks automatically by playing multiple runs.
Herbalist Collect 250 consumables by the end of wave 20
Druid		 Playing a character based around consumables, like Farmer or Glutton makes this much easier to achieve.
Unstoppable Force Hit at least 25 enemies with a single melee attack
Dwarf		 Use big slow weapons such as Spear or Anchor and hit a ton of enemies. Look for a Horde Wave. You can also use Loud. The additional enemies makes it much easier to hit 25.
Barbecue Have at least 25 enemies burning at the same time
Chef		 Use a Fireball or Flamethrower to spread a ton of fire. You can try to find a Horde wave. Pacifist is great for this, since the burning enemies don't die.
Unlucky Reroll 10 times in a single shop
Gangster		 This one can be done at the first shop. Just reroll 10 times. Pick a Character with starting harvesting like Saver for an easier time.
Overkill Deal 1000 damage to an enemy in a single hit
Ogre		 Sailor with an Anchor is quite great here. The +200% damage to cursed enemies makes it easy to deal 1000 damage.
Uncorrupted Finish a run with 0 Curse
Romantic		 Use short ranged Melee weapons and avoid killing the Sea Pig / Curse Hoarders. They give +1 Curse when killed. Avoid buying items that give curse. You can play Pacifist for an easier time. The Crash Zone map also have fewer enemies that give Curse on death.
Cautious Kill 1000 enemies that are more than 700 range away from you
Diver		 You get it over multiple games. Use weapons like Slingshot or Play Lucky/ Hunter to hit enemies all over the map.
Blind Greed Reach +100 % Pickup Range
Buccaneer		 Start a game as Soldier. You instantly get it.
